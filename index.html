Research Search Operators Guide
  • site: Search within specific domains (e.g., site:edu for educational institutions)
  • filetype: Limit to specific file types (e.g., filetype:pdf for research papers)
  • intitle: Terms in document title
  • inurl: Terms in the URL
  • author: Search for specific authors
  • Use quotes for exact phrases and - to exclude terms
  • AROUND(n) Find terms within n words of each other (e.g., climate AROUND(5) change)
